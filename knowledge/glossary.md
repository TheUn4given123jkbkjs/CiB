# Glossary

## Director

Central orchestrator responsible for managing workflow and communication between agents.

---

## Agent

An independent module with a specific responsibility inside the CiB system.

---

## Story

The raw narrative input provided by a user.

---

## Blueprint

A structured representation of a story that can be consumed by downstream agents.

The exact blueprint schema has not yet been finalized.

---

## Knowledge Base

A long-term storage system for reusable patterns, lessons, evaluations, prompts, and generated artifacts.

The exact implementation has not yet been finalized.

---

## Reflection

The process of evaluating outputs, identifying weaknesses, and producing improvement feedback.

The exact implementation has not yet been finalized.
